Valerie Bourdon is a multidisciplinary graphic designer and motion designer currently focused on branding, layout design, and 2D motion graphics. Currently employed at Sid Lee Montreal, she graduated from Dawson College with a DEC in Graphic Design and has a Bachelors degree in the Design and Computation Arts program at Concordia University.

Her wide range of experience includes exhibition/event design, interactive installations, art direction, and curation. Beyond that, her personal hobbies such as film photography, illustration, tangible circuitry, and sewing are often incorporated into her work.
