About
For the past four years, Vucko has crafted and evolved the motion identity for Google Cloud Next, shaping a system that grows stronger with every event. Our role has been to not only create moments of impact, but to create a living identity that adapts with the brand over time. Each year we build on the foundation, introducing new dimensions of adaptability, evolution, flexibility, and iteration so the identity continues to feel dynamic, relevant, and true to its essence.

This year, I took on the position of lead motion designer. My role was to coordinate the production of the motion and build the guidelines, also jumping into animation production when needed.
